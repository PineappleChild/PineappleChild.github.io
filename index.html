Dedication to my Family

Over the course of my life, my family has always been there when I needed a hand. They have showed love and kindness to me and given me support whenever I needed it the most. I look up to my parents, they are both amazing people. Their kind and hardworking personalities shape those around them positively influencing others to follow in their paths. They are there to celebrate when I succeed and there to reassure me when I fail. They have shown me that the road of life may be a difficult road filled with bumps and obstacles, but with their company and lead they showed me how to traverse this highway. I would not trade my family for anything in the world, we have our differences but at the end we are blessed to have each other and each day I feel so lucky to have such an amazing family.
